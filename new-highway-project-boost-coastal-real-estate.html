New Highway Project to Boost Coastal Real Estate Values

Inuka Properties Team December 30, 2023 4 min read

A major highway project is set to transform the coastal region of Kenya, bringing significant benefits to real estate investors and property owners. This infrastructure development is expected to increase property values, improve accessibility, and create new investment opportunities across Mombasa and Kilifi County.

Project Overview

Highway Specifications

The new highway project includes several key components:

  • Route Length: 120 kilometers of new highway
  • Lanes: 4-lane dual carriageway
  • Connections: Links Mombasa to Kilifi and Malindi
  • Timeline: 3-year construction period

Key Features

The highway will include modern infrastructure features:

  • Modern Design: International standard road design
  • Safety Features: Advanced safety systems and lighting
  • Service Areas: Rest stops and service facilities
  • Technology Integration: Smart traffic management systems

Impact on Real Estate Values

Property Value Increases

Historical data shows that highway projects typically increase property values:

  • Immediate Impact: 15-25% increase in property values
  • Long-term Growth: 30-50% appreciation over 5 years
  • Commercial Properties: Higher increases for commercial real estate
  • Residential Areas: Significant growth in residential property values

Areas Most Affected

Specific areas along the highway route will see the most significant impact:

  • Mariakani: Major intersection and commercial hub
  • Mtwapa: Growing commercial and residential area
  • Kilifi Town: County headquarters and administrative center
  • Malindi: Tourism destination with high growth potential

Investment Opportunities

Land Investment

Land along the highway route presents excellent investment opportunities:

  • Strategic Locations: Properties near highway interchanges
  • Commercial Land: Ideal for retail and office developments
  • Industrial Properties: Perfect for logistics and manufacturing
  • Residential Development: High demand for housing near the highway

Development Projects

The highway will enable various development projects:

  • Shopping Centers: Retail developments along the route
  • Office Complexes: Business parks and corporate headquarters
  • Residential Estates: Gated communities and housing projects
  • Tourism Facilities: Hotels, resorts, and entertainment venues

Economic Benefits

Business Development

The highway will stimulate business growth in the region:

  • Improved Connectivity: Better access to markets and suppliers
  • Reduced Transportation Costs: Lower logistics and shipping costs
  • Increased Trade: Enhanced trade between coastal towns
  • Job Creation: New employment opportunities in various sectors

Tourism Impact

The improved infrastructure will boost tourism:

  • Easier Access: Reduced travel time to tourist destinations
  • Increased Visitors: More tourists visiting coastal areas
  • Hotel Development: New accommodation facilities
  • Entertainment Venues: Restaurants, clubs, and recreational facilities

Timeline and Phases

Construction Phases

The project will be completed in three main phases:

  • Phase 1 (Year 1): Mombasa to Mariakani section
  • Phase 2 (Year 2): Mariakani to Kilifi section
  • Phase 3 (Year 3): Kilifi to Malindi section

Expected Completion

Key milestones and completion dates:

  • Groundbreaking: Q1 2024
  • Phase 1 Completion: Q4 2024
  • Phase 2 Completion: Q4 2025
  • Full Project Completion: Q4 2026

Investment Strategy

Early Investment Opportunities

Investors can capitalize on early opportunities:

  • Land Acquisition: Purchase land before construction begins
  • Strategic Locations: Focus on areas near planned interchanges
  • Long-term Vision: Plan for 5-10 year investment horizon
  • Diversification: Invest in different property types

Risk Management

Consider these factors when investing:

  • Timeline Delays: Potential construction delays
  • Market Volatility: Economic factors affecting property values
  • Legal Considerations: Ensure proper title and documentation
  • Professional Advice: Seek expert guidance for investment decisions

Conclusion

The new highway project represents a significant opportunity for real estate investors in coastal Kenya. With expected property value increases of 15-50% and improved accessibility, this infrastructure development will transform the region's real estate market.
