Senior Management Team

Sales and Marketing Manager

Leads our sales team and marketing initiatives, ensuring we reach the right clients with the right properties. Develops strategic marketing campaigns and maintains strong client relationships.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Sales strategy and team leadership
  • Marketing campaign development
  • Client relationship management
  • Market analysis and positioning

Finance Manager

Oversees all financial aspects of the company, from budgeting and financial planning to investment analysis and risk management. Ensures financial stability and growth.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Financial planning and budgeting
  • Investment analysis and risk management
  • Financial reporting and compliance
  • Cash flow management

Project Manager

Manages all development projects from conception to completion, ensuring quality standards are met and projects are delivered on time and within budget.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Project planning and execution
  • Quality control and standards

  • Stakeholder coordination

Compliance Manager

Ensures all company operations comply with legal and regulatory requirements, maintaining the highest standards of corporate governance and ethical business practices.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Regulatory reporting
