Corporate Social Responsibility

Building communities and creating positive impact beyond real estate

Our CSR Vision

At Inuka Properties, we believe that true success goes beyond business achievements. We are committed to creating lasting positive impact in the communities where we operate.

Beyond Business: Building Communities

Our corporate social responsibility initiatives are rooted in our core values of integrity, transparency, and accountability. We understand that as a leading real estate company, we have a responsibility to contribute to the social and economic development of our communities.

Through strategic partnerships, community engagement, and sustainable development practices, we aim to create a legacy that extends far beyond property development.

Our CSR Pillars

We focus on four key areas to maximize our positive impact on society

Education & Skills Development

Supporting educational initiatives and providing skills training opportunities for community members, especially youth and women.

Key Initiatives:

  • Scholarship programs for deserving students
  • Vocational training in construction and real estate
  • Computer literacy programs
  • Mentorship and career guidance

Healthcare & Wellness

Improving access to healthcare services and promoting wellness programs in our communities.

Key Initiatives:

  • Medical camps and health awareness programs
  • Support for local healthcare facilities
  • Clean water and sanitation projects
  • Mental health awareness campaigns

Environmental Sustainability

Implementing eco-friendly practices and supporting environmental conservation efforts.

Key Initiatives:

  • Tree planting and reforestation projects
  • Green building practices in our developments
  • Waste management and recycling programs
  • Renewable energy adoption

Community Development

Supporting infrastructure development and community empowerment initiatives.

Key Initiatives:

  • Road and bridge construction
  • Community centers and recreational facilities
  • Women and youth empowerment programs
  • Disaster relief and emergency support
