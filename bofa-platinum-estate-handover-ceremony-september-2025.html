Bofa Platinum Estate Handover Ceremony: A Milestone Event in Kilifi County

Inuka Afrika Properties invites investors and vendors to celebrate the grand handover of Bofa Platinum Estate on September 27, 2025.

Aerial view of the completed Bofa Platinum Estate controlled community development, ready for handover to our valued clients.

Inuka Afrika Properties Limited is thrilled to announce the upcoming grand handover ceremony for its prestigious Bofa Platinum Estate, scheduled for September 27, 2025. This landmark event, set to take place in the vibrant Kilifi County, marks a significant milestone for the company and its valued clients who have invested in this controlled community development.

A Celebration of Success and Community

The Bofa Platinum Estate handover ceremony is more than just a formal transfer of property; it's a celebration of successful investment, community growth, and the realization of dreams. We extend a warm invitation to all clients who have purchased properties in this development to grace the event and witness the culmination of their foresight and trust in Inuka Afrika Properties.

This event will also serve as a unique platform for various vendors to showcase their products and services, fostering local economic growth and providing attendees with a diverse range of offerings. From home improvement solutions to financial services, the event promises to be a hub of activity and opportunity.

About Bofa Platinum Estate: A Controlled Community Development

Bofa Platinum Estate stands as a testament to Inuka Afrika Properties' commitment to delivering high-quality, value-driven real estate. This controlled community development features:

  • Prime location in the rapidly developing Kilifi County
  • Well-planned infrastructure with access roads, water, and electricity
  • Proximity to essential amenities like schools, hospitals, and shopping centers
  • Secure and serene environment, ideal for residential development
  • Clear title deeds and transparent acquisition process
  • Controlled community development with architectural guidelines
  • 1-storey maisonette designs with customization options
  • Flexible 12-month payment plans for easy ownership

Design Flexibility and Customization

While Bofa Platinum Estate is a controlled community development, we understand that our clients have unique preferences. The images shown above represent our proposed architectural plans and design options. Clients have the flexibility to choose from various design elements while maintaining the overall community aesthetic and building standards.

Our architectural team works closely with each client to ensure their home reflects their personal style while adhering to the community guidelines that maintain property values and aesthetic harmony throughout the development.
