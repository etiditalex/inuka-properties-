New Highway Project to Boost Coastal Real Estate Values

Inuka Properties Team December 28, 2023
4 min read

A major highway project connecting Mombasa to Kilifi County is set to transform the coastal real estate landscape, creating unprecedented investment opportunities and significantly boosting property values along the route. This infrastructure development represents one of the most significant investments in coastal Kenya's transportation network in recent years.

The Highway Project Overview

The new highway project will create a modern, multi-lane road connecting Mombasa to key areas in Kilifi County, including Mariakani, Mtwapa, and Kilifi Town. The project includes:

  • Modern road infrastructure with multiple lanes
  • Improved connectivity between coastal towns
  • Reduced travel times between major destinations
  • Enhanced safety features and modern road design

Impact on Property Values

Infrastructure development has historically been a major driver of property value appreciation. The new highway project is expected to:

1. Increased Accessibility

Improved road connectivity will make previously remote areas more accessible, leading to:

  • Increased demand for properties along the route
  • Higher property values in newly accessible areas
  • Development of new residential and commercial zones

2. Economic Development

The highway will stimulate economic activity, resulting in:

  • New business opportunities along the route
  • Increased commercial development
  • Job creation and population growth
  • Higher demand for residential properties

3. Tourism Growth

Improved accessibility will boost tourism, leading to:

  • Increased demand for vacation rentals
  • Development of hospitality facilities
  • Higher property values in tourist areas

Investment Opportunities

The highway project creates several investment opportunities:

Areas Along the Route

Properties located along or near the new highway route are expected to see the most significant value appreciation:

  • Mariakani: Strategic location with growth potential
  • Mtwapa: Emerging tourist and residential hub
  • Kilifi Town: Administrative center with development potential
  • Areas between major towns: New development opportunities

Property Types to Consider

Different property types will benefit from the highway development:

  • Residential Properties: Increased demand for housing
  • Commercial Properties: New business opportunities
  • Land: Development potential and value appreciation
  • Hospitality Properties: Tourism growth opportunities

Timeline and Phases

The highway project is being implemented in phases:

  • Phase 1: Planning and design (Completed)
  • Phase 2: Construction of main route (In Progress)
  • Phase 3: Completion and opening (Expected 2024-2025)

Conclusion

The new highway project represents a significant opportunity for real estate investors in coastal Kenya. Properties along the route are expected to see substantial value appreciation as the project progresses and upon completion. Early investors stand to benefit the most from this infrastructure development.
